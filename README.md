### About the RPC


Macroinvertebrates play vital role for organic matter decomposition and nutrient recycling process in temperate stream. They are classified into functional feeding group (like shredder, filterers,collectors, scrapers, etc.) to acquire food in stream. The leaf litter decomposition process have started  after its entry into the stream which make leaf litter soft. The remaining solid portion of leaf litter is become the food resource of shredder in stream. Our assumption is that  organic matter   particle size is  important factor to decide macroinvertebrates population in streams.Present study aimed to check this assumption through mesocosm study.
